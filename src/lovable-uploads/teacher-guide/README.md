# Teacher Guide Resources - Module 2

This folder contains teacher guide materials and curriculum resources for Module 2: "I Am the Greatest" by James Bird.

## Available Teacher Resources

### 1. Develop/Share Your Plan Activity
- **File**: `/lovable-uploads/8cc6c170-8dc0-4741-8ab1-c32734cdd744.png`
- **Content**: Module 2 activity guide for "Develop/Share Your Plan" with teacher instructions and student guidelines
- **Duration**: 22 minutes total (15 min Develop + 7 min Share Plan)
- **Focus**: Collaborating to find evidence and pairing to share plans
- **Purpose**: Support students in creating claims with evidence both verbally and in writing

#### Activity Details
- **Teacher Flex Activity**: ThinkCERCA Best Practices Activity available
- **Support for Exceptional Needs**: Adjusted version available in Diverse Learner Guide
- **Teacher Instructions**: 
  - Explain claim requirements and evidence support
  - Guide students through reasoning/elaboration/analysis
  - Check that claims are supported with at least two reasons
  - Provide feedback on student work
- **Student Objectives**:
  - Create clear claims with supporting evidence
  - Share drafts with peers for feedback
  - Submit completed develop work as time permits

### 2. Write to Impress Activity
- **File**: `/lovable-uploads/6c91bfd8-0d1b-46e5-9e20-2d012e882b55.png`
- **Content**: Teacher guide for "Write to Impress" grammar and writing instruction
- **Focus**: Active vs. passive voice instruction and student practice
- **Purpose**: Help students understand and effectively use active voice in their writing
