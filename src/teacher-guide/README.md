# Teacher Guide Resources

This folder contains teacher guide materials and curriculum resources.

## Available Resources

### Develop/Share Your Plan Activity
- File: develop-share-plan.png
- Description: Module 2 activity guide for "Develop/Share Your Plan" with teacher instructions and student guidelines
- Purpose: Support students in creating claims with evidence both verbally and in writing

### Activity Details
- **Duration**: 22 minutes total (15 min Develop + 7 min Share Plan)
- **Focus**: Collaborating to find evidence and pairing to share plans
- **Teacher Flex Activity**: ThinkCERCA Best Practices Activity available
- **Support for Exceptional Needs**: Adjusted version available in Diverse Learner Guide

### Teacher Instructions
- Explain claim requirements and evidence support
- Guide students through reasoning/elaboration/analysis
- Check that claims are supported with at least two reasons
- Provide feedback on student work

### Student Objectives
- Create clear claims with supporting evidence
- Share drafts with peers for feedback
- Submit completed develop work as time permits
